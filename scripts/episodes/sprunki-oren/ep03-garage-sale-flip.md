# 🛒 Oren's Garage Sale Flip — Full Script (Expanded)

> **Episode**: EP03
> **Episode Type**: Single Episode — Extended (GOAL → BROKE! → HUSTLE → FAIL → GET PAID → ENJOY!)
> **Duration**: ~4-5 phút | **33 shots**
> **Characters**: Oren, Simon, Durple, Gray, Sky + American passersby
> **Locations**: Oren's bedroom → each character's home → driveway (garage sale) → bowling alley → food court
> **Theme**: Teamwork + sáng tạo biến tình huống chán thành cơ hội
> **Moral**: When everyone chips in and you add a little creativity, even $0 can turn into a great night.

## ACT 4: FAIL 2 — WIND DISASTER + THE PIVOT (Shots 20–24)

```
SHOT 20 | 3s | WS
[SFX] Sudden wind gust — "WHOOSH"; cardboard flapping; items sliding
[DIA — Oren, startled] "Yo!"
[CAM] Handheld, shaky from wind

Visual: Wide shot. A strong wind gust hits the driveway. The cardboard "GARAGE SALE" sign lifts off the recycling bin and tumbles down the sidewalk. Papers flutter. The sneakers fall off the table edge. Sky jumps off her box and chases the sign. Simon grabs the lamp before it slides. Oren stands up, hands out, trying to hold game cases down.


SHOT 21 | 3s | MS
[SFX] Small feet running on pavement — "pat pat pat"; cardboard scraping on concrete
[CAM] Handheld follow from behind, running pace

Visual: Medium shot from behind. Sky chases the cardboard sign down the sidewalk. It tumbles just ahead of her. She lunges — grabs it. The sign is bent and scuffed. She holds it up triumphantly, but the "GARAGE" part is torn. It now reads "——— SALE" with a ragged edge.


SHOT 22 | 2s | CU
[SFX] Marker cap popping — "pop"; marker squeaking on cardboard
[CAM] Static, overhead close-up on the sign

Visual: Close-up overhead shot. Oren's hands hold a fat black marker, quickly scrawling "BIG" over the torn part. The sign now reads "BIG SALE" in messy, uneven letters. A small orange thumbprint is visible on the corner.


SHOT 23 | 4s | MS
[SFX] Fingers tapping on table — rhythmic "tap-tap-tik-tap"; beatbox mouth sounds building gradually
[CAM] Handheld, pushing in slowly from the side

Visual: Medium shot from the side. Oren props the fixed sign back up, then leans on the table. Out of boredom, he starts tapping his fingers on the folding table edge in a rhythmic pattern. His head starts bobbing. His mouth begins forming beatbox sounds — a simple kick-snare pattern. His free hand taps his thigh. The rhythm builds — layering in hi-hat sounds.


SHOT 24 | 3s | MWS
[SFX] Beatbox rhythm continues — clearer now; Simon starts finger-snapping along
[DIA — Simon, grinning] "Ayyy!"
[CAM] Static, wider

Visual: Medium wide shot behind the table. Oren is fully locked in — eyes half-closed, one hand cupped to his mouth beatboxing, the other keeping rhythm on the table. Simon starts finger-snapping along. Durple taps his foot. Gray watches with a subtle head-nod. Sky smiles. The boredom has turned into a spontaneous jam session. The sound carries down the quiet suburban street.
```
