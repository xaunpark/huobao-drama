## Thể loại 5: Compilation (Multi-Arc)

### "Oren's Longest Week"

```
Arc 1: WANT(pay rent) → BROKE → HUSTLE(food delivery) → GET PAID
  ↓ [Kết nối: Eat free food samples → food coma → miss next gig]
Arc 2: COMPLICATION(lose gig) → HUSTLE(help Simon with marketing shoot) → EARN
  ↓ [Kết nối: Marketing shoot client loves Oren's beat → offers music gig]
Arc 3: CAREER OPPORTUNITY → Grind demo → Nail the pitch
  ↓ [Kết nối: Celebrate with friends]
Arc 4: ENJOY → Rent paid + music career step-up, group hangout on rooftop → "Same time next week?"
```

### "Broke But Not Broken"

```
Arc 1: WANT(anniversary dinner for Pinki) → BROKE → HUSTLE(bartending) → EARN
  ↓ [Kết nối: Learn cocktail skills → bar owner offers regular gig]
Arc 2: REGULAR GIG → Work-life balance struggle → Pinki: "I never see you"
  ↓ [Kết nối: Realize money isn't everything]
Arc 3: RESOLUTION → Plan homemade dinner instead → Pinki: "This is us" → sunset
```
