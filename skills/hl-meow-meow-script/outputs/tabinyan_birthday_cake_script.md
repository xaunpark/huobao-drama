# 🎂 Tabinyan làm bánh kem sinh nhật

**Episode Type:** Single Episode (Gift-driven)
**Duration:** ~2 phút
**Shots:** 30
**Story Beat:** GOAL → 0 YEN! → WORK → COMPLICATION → EARN → FULFILL GOAL

---

## Story Beats

| Beat | Shots | Nội dung |
|---|---|---|
| **GOAL** | 01–05 | Tabinyan thấy lịch sinh nhật Nyanko-chan → hào hứng → kiểm ví → 0 YEN! |
| **WORK** | 06–18 | Xin việc tại tiệm bánh → học làm bánh → montage nỗ lực |
| **COMPLICATION** | 19–21 | Đánh rơi quả dâu cuối cùng → chạy ra chợ mua lại |
| **EARN** | 22–24 | Nhận lương → salary ceremony |
| **FULFILL** | 25–30 | Mua nguyên liệu → hoàn thành bánh → tặng Nyanko-chan → kết ấm áp |

## Script

### — GOAL (Shots 01–05) —

```
SHOT 01 | 4s | MS
[BGM] Light ukulele plucking, lazy afternoon feel
[SFX] Pencil scratching on paper — "Kari kari"; page turning
[CAM] Static, cat-eye level, slight breathing sway

Visual: Medium shot at cat-eye level. Tabinyan sits upright at a small low table in a Japanese tatami room, center frame. She holds a pencil in one paw, flipping through a small desk calendar with the other. A heart sticker marks a date on the visible page.

SHOT 02 | 3s | CU
[SFX] Finger tap on paper — "Ton ton"
[DIA — Tabinyan, high-pitched, excited gasp] "Ara!"
[CAM] Static, slight push-in

Visual: Close-up of the calendar page. One small paw taps on a circled date with a pink heart sticker. Handwritten kanji reads "にゃんこちゃん 誕生日" (Nyanko-chan birthday).

SHOT 03 | 3s | MS
[SFX] Quick footsteps — "Pata pata pata"; fabric rustling
[DIA — Tabinyan, very high-pitched, determined chirp] "Meow!"
[CAM] Handheld follow, panning right as Tabinyan jumps up

Visual: Medium shot. Tabinyan springs up from the table, stands straight with both paws clenched in fists at chest level, eyes wide and sparkling. She turns right toward a small shelf where a cat-shaped coin purse sits.

SHOT 04 | 3s | CU
[SFX] Coin purse snap open — "Pachi"; shake shake — silence; coin purse turned upside down — nothing falls
[DIA — Tabinyan, pitch dropping, deflated] "Ara...?"
[CAM] Static, cat-eye level, tight on paws

Visual: Close-up of two small paws holding open a cat-shaped coin purse, center frame. The purse is completely empty. One paw flips it upside down and shakes it. Nothing falls out.

SHOT 05 | 3s | CU
[SFX] Sad wind sound — "Hyuu~"
[CAM] Static, cat-eye level, slight tilt down

Visual: Close-up of Tabinyan's face, center frame. Mouth turned down, ears slightly flattened, eyes looking down at the empty purse in her paws. She blinks twice slowly.
```

### — WORK: Xin việc & Học làm bánh (Shots 06–18) —

```
SHOT 06 | 4s | WS
[BGM] Tempo shifts — cheerful, bouncy acoustic
[SFX] Street ambience, distant chatter, shop bells — "Chirin chirin"
[CAM] Handheld follow from behind, walking pace, natural bob

Visual: Wide shot at cat-eye level. Tabinyan walks upright along a narrow Japanese shotengai street, moving away from camera toward a small bakery (洋菓子屋) with a glass display window. A "パート募集" (part-time help wanted) sign hangs in the window. Morning shoppers pass by.

SHOT 07 | 3s | MWS
[SFX] Door chime — "Karan koron"; tiny footsteps on tile floor
[DIA — Tabinyan, polite, high-pitched] "Meow!"
[DIA — Baker, warm female voice, Keigo] "Ara, irasshaimase~"
[HUMAN REACTION: gentle_mentor]
[CAM] Static, slightly low angle from inside the shop, looking toward the entrance

Visual: Medium wide two-shot from inside the bakery. A middle-aged Japanese woman in a white apron and chef hat stands behind a flour-dusted counter, left of frame. Tabinyan pushes through the glass door, right of frame, standing upright. The baker bends down to Tabinyan's eye level, hands on knees, smiling.

SHOT 08 | 3s | MS
[SFX] Apron fabric rustling — "Shuru shuru"; tie knot — "Kyu"
[HUMAN REACTION: gentle_mentor]
[CAM] Static, cat-eye level, slight breathing sway

Visual: Medium shot. The baker kneels behind Tabinyan, tying a miniature white apron around her. Tabinyan stands straight, arms slightly out to the sides, looking down at the apron. Shelves of pastries and cake molds fill the background.

SHOT 09 | 3s | MS
[SFX] Egg cracking — "Paki"; shell pieces falling — "Karan"
[HUMAN REACTION: gentle_mentor]
[CAM] Static, eye-level, framing both at counter height

Visual: Medium shot, side angle. The baker demonstrates cracking an egg one-handed into a steel mixing bowl on the counter, left of frame. Tabinyan watches intently from a step stool, right of frame, leaning forward with both paws gripping the counter edge.

SHOT 10 | 3s | CU
[SFX] Egg cracking — "Paki paki"; shell crumbling; yolk splashing — "Becha"
[DIA — Tabinyan, focused grunt] "Mmm~!"
[CAM] Static, overhead

Visual: Close-up top-down shot. Two small paws grip an egg over a mixing bowl. The egg cracks unevenly, shell fragments fall into the batter along with the yolk. Small bits of shell float on the surface.

SHOT 11 | 2s | INSERT
[SFX] Chopsticks picking — "Tsun tsun"
[HUMAN REACTION: amused_observer]
[CAM] Static, overhead, tight

Visual: Insert shot, top-down. The baker's large fingers delicately pick shell fragments out of the batter with chopsticks while Tabinyan's paw holds the bowl steady from the edge.

SHOT 12 | 3s | MS
[SFX] Electric mixer whirring — "Uii~n"; batter splashing
[CAM] Handheld, slight vibration matching mixer

Visual: Medium shot, front angle. Tabinyan stands on a step stool, gripping a hand mixer with both paws. The mixer vibrates visibly. Batter spins in the bowl. Small drops of batter dot her apron and the counter surface.

SHOT 13 | 2s | CU
[SFX] Mixer whirring continues; batter thickening — "Neba neba"
[CAM] Static, overhead, tight

Visual: Close-up top-down of the mixing bowl. Creamy yellow batter swirls smoothly. Two paws hold the mixer handle steady in frame.

SHOT 14 | 3s | MS
[SFX] Batter pouring into tin — "Toro~"; tin placed on rack — "Katan"
[CAM] Static, cat-eye level, slight pan right following movement

Visual: Medium shot, side angle. Tabinyan tilts a mixing bowl carefully, pouring batter into a round cake tin on the counter. She sets the bowl down and uses a small spatula to scrape the last of the batter. The baker stands behind, arms crossed, nodding.

SHOT 15 | 3s | MS
[SFX] Oven door opening — "Gi~"; tin sliding on rack — "Shaa"; oven door closing — "Batan"
[CAM] Static, low angle from floor level, looking up at oven

Visual: Medium shot from low angle. Tabinyan pushes the cake tin into a professional oven using both paws with oven mitts. The baker's hand rests on the oven door handle above. Orange glow from inside the oven illuminates Tabinyan's face.

SHOT 16 | 3s | MS
[SFX] Cream piping bag squeezing — "Nyu nyu nyu~"
[CAM] Handheld, slight push-in, cat-eye level

Visual: Medium shot, front angle. Tabinyan stands on a step stool at the decorating station, squeezing a piping bag with both paws, applying white cream rosettes along the edge of a round sponge cake on a turntable. Her tongue sticks out slightly in concentration.

SHOT 17 | 2s | INSERT
[SFX] Cream piping — "Nyu~"; turntable spinning — "Kuru kuru"
[CAM] Static, overhead

Visual: Insert top-down shot. The cake rotates slowly on the turntable. White cream rosettes form an even ring around the top edge. Two small paws hold the piping bag tip at a precise angle.

SHOT 18 | 3s | CU
[SFX] Strawberry placed — "Poton"; Tabinyan's satisfied exhale
[DIA — Tabinyan, proud whisper] "Mm~!"
[CAM] Static, cat-eye level, slight push-in

Visual: Close-up of Tabinyan's face, center frame. She places a bright red strawberry on top of the cake with one paw, looking down. Her eyes narrow with satisfaction, a small proud smile forming.
```

### — EARN: Salary Ceremony (Shots 22–24) —

```
SHOT 22 | 4s | WS
[BGM] Warm strings swell, gentle and dignified
[SFX] Clock ticking; evening ambience — cicadas, distant train
[CAM] Static, cat-eye level, wide framing

Tabinyan stands opposite, at attention, paws at her sides. A clean white envelope sits on the counter between them.

SHOT 23 | 3s | MWS

Visual: Medium wide two-shot. The baker bows deeply (ojigi) and extends the white salary envelope with both hands toward Tabinyan across the counter. Tabinyan reaches up with both paws to receive it, bowing her head in return.

SHOT 24 | 3s | CU
[SFX] Envelope clutched to chest — "Gyu"; tiny exhale of relief
[DIA — Tabinyan, very high-pitched, triumphant] "Yatta!"
[CAM] Static, cat-eye level, slight breathing sway

Visual: Close-up of Tabinyan's face and upper body. She presses the salary envelope against her chest with both paws, eyes closed, head tilted up slightly. A wide, relieved grin spreads across her face.
```

```
SHOT 28 | 4s | MWS
[SFX] Door sliding open — "Gara"; tiny footsteps; surprised gasp

SHOT 29 | 4s | CU

SHOT 30 | 5s | MWS
[BGM] Final warm swell, ukulele and soft strings resolving
[SFX] Two small bodies sitting down — "Ston ston"; fork clinking on plate — "Chin"; chewing — "Mogu mogu"
[DIA — Tabinyan, soft, content] "Purr~"
[CAM] Static, low angle from floor, looking up at them, slow gentle zoom out

Visual: Medium wide shot, slightly low angle. Tabinyan and Nyanko-chan sit side by side on cushions at the low table. The cake sits between them with one slice cut and placed on a small plate. Nyanko-chan holds a tiny fork in one paw, eating. Tabinyan leans slightly toward her, eyes half-closed, content. Through the window behind them, the sky shows warm evening colors.
```
